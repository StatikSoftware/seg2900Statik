Tools To Simplify The Cooking Process

Simr is filled with loads of features that make tricky cooking processes simple! After picking from the wide variety of curated recipes we've made available, Simr guides you through the entire cooking process from start to finish.


With our handy ingredients list, you can check off every ingredient you already have in your kitchen. If you're missing ingredients, Simr suggests an alternative to this ingredient, and provides the nearest location where you can purchase your missing ingredients.


Once you have all your ingredients, each recipe is broken down into a step by step process designed for the most helpless of cooks. Through Simr we've made several cooking tutorials available. These tutorials cover all the common cooking practices which could be found in our recipes, such as dough kneading, sauteing or even flambaying!

Social Media Synergy

We understand that one of the best parts of cooking is being able to brag about your cooking skills on social media! Naturally, Simr allows you to take pictures of your fantastic creations and share them to a wide variety of social media platforms.


Simr even tracks how many likes your shared pictures recieve! Ultimately, more likes allows you to unlock more recipes and other perks through Simr's gamification system.

Gamification

Simr makes conventional cooking fun! In making Simr we strive to making cooking an enjoyable experience for all ages. This is done through rewards, challenges, encouragement and overall GAMIFICATION!


Additionally, by sharing your cooking experience through social media, or just by using Simr, you gain experience points. These experience points allow you to unlock new recipes, challenges and app layout customisations.
